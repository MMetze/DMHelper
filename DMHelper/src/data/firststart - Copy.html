First Steps

To get started, here's a few ideas:

  1. Dive in: click "New Campaign", then add a few PCs (or import them from D&D Beyond) and add a map or combat and off you go

  2. Join our Discord server, where you will find lots of friendly Helpers: https://discord.gg/Uzum3mCeDG
  3. Register for our Community Forums and grab the (free) Fan Content Bestiary
  4. Our single most Frequency Asked Question is how to make the Player's Window full-screen: Just press "F"!
  5. Check out the Getting Started Guide (link in the app)
